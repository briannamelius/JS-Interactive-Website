About Us: Lewis and Clark

We are a vocational school open to all Juniors and Seniors in the St. Charles County. We offer a large variety of programs - from Traditional automotive and welding, to programs in all areas of residential construction, to health occupations, retail, and IT. Lewis and Clark offers something for nearly everyone!
